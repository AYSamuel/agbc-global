Welcome → Home branch → Language
Pick home branch (Glasgow/Berlin/Emmen/Ogbomosho) and language (EN/DE/NL). Stored on device: no sign-up yet. Notification permission is asked later, in context (docs 06), never here.

03 · ENTER APP AS GUEST
Full browse, zero friction
Home feed, watch any branch, read testimonies & the map, see events, service times, and give: all without an account.
04 · THE ACCOUNT GATE
Sign in only when you go from watching to participating
A soft prompt appears the first time you try to:
Post a testimony Submit / stand with a prayer Give glory 🙌 Track your rhythm RSVP Register for a course
◆ DECISION 1 · AUTH METHOD
Phone OTP only · WhatsApp-first delivery
Decided (docs 03): phone number + one-time code is the ONLY v1 sign-in; the code arrives via WhatsApp first with SMS fallback (reliable and familiar in Nigeria). No passwords. Google/Apple are post-v1 options. Profile after: name, photo, confirm home branch + language, 16+ confirmation.
Settled 2026-07-12; revisit secondary sign-ins post-v1 only.
05 · The loops that bring people back
The Sunday loop
Notify before service → attend or watch live → tap I'm here → rhythm +1 → milestone celebrated & shareable.
The testimony loop
Read a testimony → give glory 🙌 → share to WhatsApp → inspired to post your own → moderated → published to the family & the map.
The prayer → testimony loop
Post a request → others stand with you (live count) → mark Answered → one tap turns it into a testimony. Prayer feeds the wall.

◆ DECISION 2 · MODERATION
Testimonies & prayers are public: who approves?
My rec: new posts go to a pending queue; a branch leader approves before it's public. Anonymous posting allowed. Report button on everything.
Or: auto-publish + report-only (faster, riskier)?

Controls
Per-category opt-in · targeted by home branch & language · quiet hours · whole-family vs my-branch.
◆ DECISION 3 · DELIVERY
My rec: push as primary (one provider across iOS+Android), + share-to-WhatsApp for anything a member wants to pass on. No SMS in v1.
