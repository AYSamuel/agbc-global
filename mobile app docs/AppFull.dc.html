Grace that carries you
“My grace is sufficient for you, for my power is made perfect in weakness.”
2 Corinthians 12:9
There are seasons when your own strength runs out — and that is exactly where grace begins. Paul did not pray for a lighter load; he discovered a greater supply.
Today, name the thing you cannot carry. Then hand it over. Grace is not a reward for the strong; it is a gift for the honest.
